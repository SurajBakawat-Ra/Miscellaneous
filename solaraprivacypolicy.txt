# Privacy Policy

## Introduction
Welcome to SOLARA. This privacy policy explains how SOLARA handles user information and data when you use our Android games. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

## Information We Collect
### 1. Information Automatically Collected
- **AdMob Data:** We use Google AdMob to serve advertisements in our games. AdMob may collect data about your device, your interaction with ads, and other information as outlined in Google's privacy policies. SOLARA does not collect or store this data.

## How We Use Information
- **Ad Serving:** The information collected by AdMob is used to serve personalized advertisements within our games. This helps us keep our games free to play and provide a better user experience.

## Third-Party Services
### AdMob
- **Google AdMob:** We integrate Google AdMob to display ads in our games. AdMob may collect and use data as described in the [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). This may include device information, ad performance metrics, and user interactions with ads.

## Your Choices
- **Ad Preferences:** You can manage your ad preferences and opt out of personalized ads through your device settings or by visiting the [Google Ads Settings](https://adssettings.google.com).

## Data Security
We take reasonable measures to protect the information that is processed on our behalf by AdMob. However, please be aware that no method of transmitting information over the internet or storing information is completely secure.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within our games or on our website. Your continued use of our games after any changes indicates your acceptance of the new privacy policy.
